## 4. Automated Insight Generation using LLMs

### Topics Covered

- Converting Model Outputs into Human Language
- AI-generated Reports
- Automated Decision Support
- Business Intelligence with LLMs

### Example

Input:

```text
Prediction: High Risk
Top Features:
- Age
- Blood Pressure
- Glucose
```

Output:

```text
The model predicts that the individual belongs to the high-risk category.
The prediction is primarily influenced by elevated glucose levels,
blood pressure, and age-related factors.
```
